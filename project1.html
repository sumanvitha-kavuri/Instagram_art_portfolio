Inspired By

My artworks are inspired by the rich tapestry of Indian heritage and the timeless beauty of feminine grace. Through my creations, I celebrate the strength, elegance, and cultural depth of Indian women. I aim to showcase the powerful essence of femininity, rooted in the wisdom and traditions that have shaped India's cultural identity. My work honors women as symbols of grace, strength, and resilience within the rich heritage of India.

Achievements

In 2017, I was honored to win the national-level painting competition organized by the PCRA (Petroleum Conservation Research Association). This achievement earned me a National Level Certificate and was a significant milestone in my artistic career. My artwork for the competition focused on the theme of energy conservation, combining creativity with social impact. This recognition continues to inspire me and motivates my ongoing pursuit of artistic excellence.

Experience

During my time in college, I worked as a Part-Time Portrait Artist, where I had the opportunity to hone my skills in creating detailed and expressive portraits. This experience taught me how to capture the nuances of human emotion and expression, refining my technique and attention to detail. Working with a variety of clients, I developed a deeper understanding of portraiture, which has become one of my strongest artistic specialties.
